## About Me

I am currently a PhD student at [The Chinese University of Hong Kong](https://www.cuhk.edu.hk/), working on AI-powered healthcare applications and multimodal learning. Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant at [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology](https://hkust.edu.hk/).

I received my Bachelor's degree in Automation from [University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC)](https://www.uestc.edu.cn/) in June 2024, where I graduated with **Honored Research Degree** and **Outstanding Graduate** distinctions. During my undergraduate studies, I maintained a GPA of **3.99/4.0** (ranked **2/278**).

## Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of:
- **Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare**: Medical image analysis, ECG monitoring, Traditional Chinese Medicine
- **Multimodal Learning**: Cross-modal translation, visual-language models
- **Time Series Analysis**: Forecasting, anomaly detection, signal processing
- **Computer Vision**: Image classification, object detection, transfer learning
